Face care tips

Always treat facial skin with extra care, especially the delicate eye area. Try to avoid ingredients such as:

  • Fragrance (even essential oils), as these can sensitize the skin.
  • Harsh detergents such as SLS and SLES.
  • Alcohol based products, as these can dry the skin.
  • Physical exfoliators; these can cause micro-tears in the skin, which leads to accelerated aging.

Choose healthy alternatives from our curated list.

Body care tips

Whenever possible avoid the following ingredients.

  • DMDM Hydantoin (formaldehyde donor and probable human carcinogen)
  • Aluminium chlorohydrate (antiperspirant associated with heavy metal toxicity)
  • Talc (Anti-caking agent; often contaminated with asbestos)
  • Sulfate based surfactants (often contaminated with the known human carcinogen; ethylene oxide)

Instead, opt for some of our hand-picked favorites!

Hair care tips

Always choose extra gentle products to care for your hair and scalp. Ingredients to be avoided include:

  • Sulfate based surfactants, which can strip and damage the scalp
  • Quaternary amonium salts (quats) can cause heavy build up on the hair and scalp
  • Talc found in many dry shampoos (Again, is often contaminated with asbestos)
